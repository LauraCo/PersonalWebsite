Underwater Hockey

I play for Aberystwyth University’s team, and I’m also their Social Secretary, but in the past I’ve Captained Wales Under19s and briefly trained with Great Britain Under19s Ladies.

But what is Underwater Hockey?

It's a fast-paced, high adrenaline sport, played on the bottom of the pool with short sticks and a 1.5kg Puck. There are teams of 10 players, 6 playing, 4 substitutes, and the aim is to work together to score a goal in the opponents goal at the opposite end of the pool.

For more information, see Aberystwyth University's Underwater Hockey's Youtube page!
